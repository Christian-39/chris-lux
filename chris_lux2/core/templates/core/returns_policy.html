Returns & Exchanges

Our commitment to quality and your satisfaction

Return Window

We offer a 14-day return policy. You have 14 days after receiving your item to request a return. To be eligible for a return, your item must be in the same condition that you received it: unworn or unused, with tags, and in its original packaging.

Hygiene & Safety Requirements

Strict Hygiene Policy

Due to the nature of hair products and health regulations, we cannot accept returns if the security seal is broken or if the hair has been brushed, washed, dyed, or installed. All hair must be in its original bundle ties.

The Return Process

1

Contact us at {{ site_settings.contact_email|default:"returns@chris-lux.com" }} with your order number and reason for return.

2

If your return is accepted, we'll send you a return shipping label and detailed instructions via email.

3

Pack the item securely in its original packaging and drop it off at the designated carrier location.

4

Once received and inspected (within 3-5 business days), we will notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

Refunds

If approved, you'll be automatically refunded on your original payment method within 10 business days. Please remember it can take some time for your bank or credit card company to process and post the refund to your account.
