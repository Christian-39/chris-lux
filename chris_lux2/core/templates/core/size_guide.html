Size & Length Guide

Ensure the perfect fit for your luxury hair and wigs

Hair Length Chart

Please note that the length of our hair is measured when straight. For wavy or curly textures, the hair will appear 2-3 inches shorter than the actual length.

Length (Inches) Where it Falls Perfect For
10" - 12" Chin / Shoulder Length Bobs & Lobs
14" - 16" Armpit Length Natural Volume
18" - 22" Mid-Back / Bra Strap Classic Glamour
24" - 30" Waist / Hip Length Extra Drama
Pro Tip: If you are tall, the hair may appear shorter on your frame. We recommend going 2 inches longer than your target look.

Wig Cap Measurements

To find your size, measure the circumference of your head starting from the front hairline, behind your ears, to the nape of your neck.

Small 21" - 21.5"
Medium (Average) 22" - 22.5"
Large 23" - 23.5"

All our wigs come with adjustable straps and combs for a secure fit.

Texture vs. Length

Straight

True to length. Falls exactly where measured without any retraction.

Wavy

Retracts about 1-2 inches due to the natural 'S' pattern formation.

Curly/Kinky

Retracts 2-4 inches depending on the tightness of the curl pattern.
