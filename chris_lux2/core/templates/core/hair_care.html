How to Maintain Your Extensions

To ensure your Chris Lux hair lasts as long as possible and maintains its luxurious quality, follow these expert care tips:

Gentle Detangling

Always detangle from ends to roots using a wide-tooth comb or specialized extension brush before washing or styling.

Sulfate-Free Cleansing

Use only sulfate-free shampoos and silicone-free conditioners. Wash gently in lukewarm water, avoiding circular motions that cause tangling.

Heat Protection

Avoid excessive heat styling. When using hot tools, always apply a quality heat protectant and keep temperatures below 350°F (175°C).

Nighttime Care

Braid or wrap hair before sleep. Use a silk or satin pillowcase to minimize friction and prevent dryness.

Pro Tip

Deep condition your extensions every 2-3 weeks using a moisturizing hair mask. This maintains softness and prevents the ends from becoming dry or brittle.
