Our Story

Redefining Beauty Through Premium Hair

Founded in Lagos, Nigeria, Chris Lux and Accessories was born from a passion for helping women feel confident and beautiful. We believe that every woman deserves access to high-quality, luxurious hair that looks and feels natural.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide authentic, ethically sourced human hair at accessible prices. Today, we're proud to be one of Nigeria's leading luxury hair brands, serving customers nationwide and beyond.
