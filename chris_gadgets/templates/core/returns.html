Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we offer a straightforward return process.

Return Window: You have 7 days from the date of delivery to initiate a return.

Eligible Items

Items can be returned if they meet the following conditions:

  • Item is unused and in original condition
  • All original packaging is intact
  • All accessories, manuals, and documentation are included
  • Item is not damaged (unless received damaged)

Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Software and digital products (once activated)
  • Personalized or customized items
  • Gift cards and vouchers
  • Items marked as "Final Sale"
  • Items damaged due to misuse

How to Return

1
Request Return

Log into your account and request a return from your order history

2
Pack Item

Pack the item securely in its original packaging with all accessories

3
Ship Item

Ship the item to our return address (shipping cost is your responsibility)

4
Receive Refund

Once received and inspected, your refund will be processed

Note: Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error or a defective product.

Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item:

  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  • Provide photos of the damage/defect
  • We will arrange a free pickup and replacement
  • No return shipping costs for damaged/defective items

Exchanges

We currently do not offer direct exchanges. If you need a different item:

  1. Return the original item following our return process
  2. Place a new order for the desired item
