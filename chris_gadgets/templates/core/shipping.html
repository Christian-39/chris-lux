Processing Time

All orders are processed within 24 hours of payment verification:

  • Orders placed before 12 PM: Processed same day
  • Orders placed after 12 PM: Processed next business day
  • Weekend orders: Processed on Monday

Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive:

  • Email notification with tracking number
  • SMS updates on delivery status
  • Access to track your order in your account

You can track your order by:

  1. Logging into your account
  2. Going to "Order History"
  3. Clicking "Track Order" on your order

Delivery Process

Order Placed

You place your order and complete payment

Payment Verified

We verify your payment (usually within 24 hours)

Order Processed

Your order is packed and prepared for shipping

Shipped

Your order is on its way to you

Delivered

Your order arrives at your specified address

Important Notes

  • Please ensure your delivery address is accurate and complete
  • Someone must be available to receive the package
  • Delivery may be attempted up to 3 times
  • Failed deliveries may incur additional charges
  • Rural areas may have extended delivery times
